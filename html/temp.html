    Latitude vs. Max Temperature.

    As one can expect, the is a correlation between a location's latitude and the max temperature. As the latitude gets closer to the equator at 0° the temperature goes up. However it is worth noting when data was selected, May 5th 2019. At this time of year the Northern Hemisphere (positive degrees latitude) the temperature is going up and in the southern hemisphere (negative degrees latitude) the temperature is beginning to go down.
