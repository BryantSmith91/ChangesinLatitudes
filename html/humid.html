    Latitude vs. Humidity.

         The above plot shows that there is very little correlation between a city's latitude and it's % humidity. Surprisingly the areas with the lowest humidity are actually closest to the equator. However, between 10° latitude and -60° latitude very few cities sampled have a humidity less than 60, and even fewer are less than 40% humidity. This can likely be attributed to the time of year where the southern hemisphere is coming out of the summer and transitioning into the autumn.
